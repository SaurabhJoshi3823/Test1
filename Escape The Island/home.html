Once upon a time, there was a small plane flying over the ocean with a group of passengers on board. Suddenly, the plane experienced some engine trouble, and before anyone knew it, it spiraled out of control, crashing into the ocean below. Most of the passengers went missing upon impact, but Mark, a young man traveling alone to visit his family on a nearby island, was lucky enough to survive. As he emerged from the wreckage, he realized that he was stranded on a deserted island. The island was completely unknown to him, with no signs of civilization or any other beings around. He knew that he had to find a way to survive until he could be rescued. Mark started to explore the island, gathering useful items along the way. He came across some options that he needed to carefully consider before acting upon them.
